Operating between Canada and Colombia, I blend years of industry experience with an unwavering passion for storytelling and design, drawing inspiration from the rich culltural heritage and vibrant creativity of both countries. In Colombia, where art and design are deeply woven into the fabric of everyday life, I find fresh perspectives that fuel my work, bringing a unique blend of Latin American warmth and innovation to everything I create. Whether you're a large studio with global ambitions or a local business carving out your niche, I’m here to craft visuals that not only capture attention but leave a lasting, meaningful impression. My goal is to help you tell your story in a way that resonates deeply with your audience, building a connection that transcends borders and stands the test of time.

Achivements

I’ve had the thrilll of collaborating with industry giants like Bardel Entertainment, where I contributed as a 2D Animator and Compositor on Emmy-winning shows like Rick and Morty (Season 4) and The Dragon Prince (Seasons 1-3). The excitement of bringing those iconic moments to life is something I’ll never forget.

As a creator and director at Blumm Brand, I’ve pushed creative boundaries, leading award-winning short films that have earned global recognition.. One of the highlights? Winning the prestigious 2023 Lápiz de Acero International Colombian Design Award for Best Animation. It’s an honor to see my work resonate on such a wide scale, and I’m just getting started!
